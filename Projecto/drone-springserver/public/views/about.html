Quem somos It's Nice to Meet You!

We are a team of students from University of Aveiro working in the final project of our degree.

The work is about monitoring parks using drones. This solution was implemented for the University of Aveiro, but built thinking of the possible interchangeability with other places and even systems.

The system works with the help of a drone and all the necessary technologies to find free parking lots and to do statistical analysis. The information is then compiled and displayed to users using this friendly interface.
